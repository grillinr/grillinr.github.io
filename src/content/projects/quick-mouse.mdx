Quick Mouse explores low-latency input streaming from phone sensors to desktop cursor control.

## Challenges

- Noise smoothing for gyroscope input.
- Consistent feel across Windows, macOS, and Linux.
- Clear pairing flow between devices.

## Future improvements

- Gesture macros.
- Adaptive sensitivity based on movement patterns.

NQ brings together books, movies, TV, and games into a shared queue experience.

## What I focused on

- Modeling media relationships in neo4j.
- Keeping API shape predictable with GraphQL.
- Designing for cross-platform discovery rather than single-app tracking.

## Next iteration ideas

- Better ranking strategy based on completion history.
- Shared queues for friend groups.
- Lightweight importers from existing watchlists.

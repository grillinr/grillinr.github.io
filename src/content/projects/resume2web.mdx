Resume2Web parses resume files and transforms them into editable web portfolio scaffolds.

## Core flow

1. Upload PDF or text resume.
2. Extract structured sections.
3. Generate an interactive website users can customize.

The goal is to reduce friction between "I have a resume" and "I have a polished online presence."
